Our Facilities

We provide a range of modern amenities to ensure a comfortable and convenient stay for all our residents.

Kitchen & Dining

Our well-equipped kitchen area provides basic facilities for light cooking, including a stove and essential utensils. We ensure access to clean and safe drinking water with our 24/7 RO purified water supply, promoting a healthy living environment.

  • Stove & Utensils Provided for light cooking
  • RO purified drinking water available 24/7

Laundry & Cleaning

We prioritize cleanliness and convenience. Our dedicated staff ensures daily cleaning of common areas and rooms. A laundry facility is available for your clothes, making your stay hassle-free.

  • Daily cleaning services
  • Laundry facility included (for clothes, excluding undergarments)

Toilets & Bathrooms

Our PG is equipped with both Indian and Western style toilets to cater to diverse preferences. All bathrooms are regularly cleaned and maintained to ensure hygiene and comfort.

  • Indian and Western style toilets available

Safety & Security

Your safety is our top priority. Our premises are secured with 24/7 CCTV surveillance, providing continuous monitoring. Additionally, a full-time caretaker is available on-site to assist residents and ensure a secure living environment.

  • 24/7 CCTV surveillance for enhanced security
  • Full-time caretaker on premises

Parking

We offer dedicated two-wheeler parking facilities for our residents, ensuring convenience and security for your vehicles.

  • Dedicated two-wheeler parking available
